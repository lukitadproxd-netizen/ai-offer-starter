# Discovery Questions

## Customer Context

1. What are you trying to improve right now?
2. How do you handle this today?
3. What makes the current process annoying or expensive?

## Problem

4. How often does this happen?
5. What happens if it is not solved?
6. Who else is affected?

## Buying Fit

7. Have you paid for something similar before?
8. What would make this worth paying for?
9. What would make you reject it?

## Delivery

10. What information can you safely share?
11. Who needs to review the final deliverable?
12. What would a successful handoff look like?
