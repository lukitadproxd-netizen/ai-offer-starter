# Tourism Provider Report

## Customer

Small tourism providers such as cabins, guides, hosterias, and local experiences.

## Problem

Owners do not always know how their public presence looks to travelers across listings, search results, photos, FAQs, and booking copy.

## Offer

A concise public-presence report:

- public listing review
- booking copy notes
- FAQ gaps
- photo and trust signal observations
- prioritized improvement checklist

## Outcome

The provider receives a clear list of practical improvements they can review and implement.

## Exclusions

- no guaranteed bookings
- no private account access
- no fake reviews
- no ad management
