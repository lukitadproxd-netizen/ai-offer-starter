# WhatsApp Sales OS

## Customer

Small local businesses that receive repeated sales or booking questions through WhatsApp.

## Problem

Replies are slow, inconsistent, and hard to follow up. The owner repeats the same answers and loses track of interested leads.

## Offer

A lightweight WhatsApp sales operating system:

- common question response kit
- lead status labels
- follow-up message templates
- simple handoff guide
- weekly review checklist

## Outcome

The business can respond more consistently and follow up with less manual confusion.

## Exclusions

- no guaranteed sales
- no spam automation
- no scraping
- no unofficial WhatsApp automation that violates platform rules
