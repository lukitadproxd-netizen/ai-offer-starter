# Local SEO Pack Validation Plan

## Discovery Questions

- What do customers ask before booking?
- Which public profile receives the most attention?
- What information changes often?
- Have you paid for local marketing help before?

## Pilot

Offer a small audit for one public profile and one service page.

## Success Criteria

The customer says the recommendations are specific enough to implement or pays for the full pack.

## Safety

Do not promise search ranking changes. Do not create fake signals.
