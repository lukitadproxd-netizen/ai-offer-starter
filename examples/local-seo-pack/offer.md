# Local SEO Pack

## Customer

Local service businesses with incomplete or unclear public search presence.

## Problem

Potential customers cannot quickly understand services, location, opening hours, photos, FAQs, or trust signals.

## Offer

A local SEO clarity pack:

- public profile audit
- service description rewrite
- FAQ suggestions
- photo checklist
- review response templates

## Outcome

The business has clearer public information and a prioritized update checklist.

## Exclusions

- no ranking guarantee
- no fake reviews
- no review gating
- no spam backlinks
