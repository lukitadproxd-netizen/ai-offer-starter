# Mini Store Delivery Checklist

- [ ] Confirm product list and categories.
- [ ] Collect customer-approved product details and images.
- [ ] Draft product descriptions.
- [ ] Build page or no-code catalog.
- [ ] Add order inquiry flow.
- [ ] Test mobile readability.
- [ ] Confirm no unsupported payment claims.
- [ ] Handoff editing notes.
- [ ] Ask for feedback after first customer use.
