# Mini Store

## Customer

Local sellers with a small product catalog who need a simple online ordering page.

## Problem

Products are shared through messages or scattered posts, making it hard for customers to see options and ask clearly.

## Offer

A mini store package:

- product list structure
- simple landing page copy
- product descriptions
- order inquiry flow
- handoff guide

## Outcome

Customers can browse the offer more clearly and send better purchase inquiries.

## Exclusions

- no payment processing unless separately agreed
- no guaranteed sales
- no inventory management system
