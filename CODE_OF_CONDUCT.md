# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want `ai-offer-starter` to be a practical, respectful, and safe open source project.

Contributors and maintainers agree to participate in a way that is welcoming, honest, and focused on useful work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent when possible.
- Give feedback on the work, not the person.
- Keep examples realistic and ethical.
- Respect privacy and consent.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or imagery.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Promoting scams, spam, or deceptive sales tactics.
- Repeatedly pushing income claims or manipulative patterns after maintainers say no.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or block participants who violate this code of conduct.

## Reporting

If you see behavior that violates this code, contact the maintainers listed in `MAINTAINERS.md`.
