# Validation

Validation means checking whether a real customer cares before you build too much.

## What to Validate

- customer exists
- problem is real
- problem is urgent enough
- proposed deliverable is understandable
- price range is plausible
- outreach channel is ethical
- delivery is feasible

## Lightweight Validation Methods

- interview 3 to 5 people in the market
- show a one-page offer
- ask for feedback on the problem, not praise for the idea
- offer a small paid pilot
- ask what they use today

## Validation Is Not

- asking friends if an idea is cool
- sending bulk messages
- promising a result you cannot control
- building a full app before talking to anyone
