# Outreach Ethics

Outreach should be specific, respectful, and easy to ignore.

## Ethical Outreach Rules

- Personalize based on public context or prior relationship.
- Mention one observed problem.
- Offer one small next step.
- Avoid fake urgency.
- Avoid guaranteed outcomes.
- Do not scrape private contact lists.
- Do not send repeated unwanted follow-ups.

## Good Message Shape

1. Short greeting.
2. Specific observation.
3. Relevant possible outcome.
4. Low-pressure call to action.
5. Respectful close.

Use `buildOutreachMessage()` or `templates/outreach-message.md`.

## Stop Conditions

Stop contacting someone if they decline, do not respond after a reasonable follow-up, or if your message would need exaggeration to sound compelling.
