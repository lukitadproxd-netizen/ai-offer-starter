# Problem Selection

A strong offer solves a problem the customer already recognizes.

## Strong Problems

Look for problems that are:

- frequent
- annoying or expensive
- easy to explain
- attached to an existing workflow
- possible to improve with a small deliverable

## Weak Problems

Avoid problems that are:

- vague
- invented by the seller
- only exciting because they mention AI
- impossible to measure
- dependent on a guaranteed business outcome

## Problem Test

Ask:

- Does the customer already complain about this?
- What do they do today instead?
- What happens if they ignore it?
- Can a small scoped deliverable improve it?
- Can I deliver safely without private data?
