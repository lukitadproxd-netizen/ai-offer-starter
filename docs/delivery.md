# Delivery

Delivery is where trust is earned. A small offer delivered clearly is better than a large offer delivered vaguely.

## Delivery Flow

1. Confirm scope and success criteria.
2. Collect only the information needed.
3. Build the deliverables.
4. Review for accuracy, usability, and ethical claims.
5. Handoff files and usage notes.
6. Provide the agreed support window.
7. Ask for feedback.
8. Record improvements for the next version.

## Quality Checks

- Does the deliverable match the promised scope?
- Are AI-generated outputs reviewed by a human?
- Are claims accurate and modest?
- Can the customer use the deliverable without you?
- Are next steps documented?

Use `createDeliveryChecklist()` or `templates/delivery-checklist.md`.
