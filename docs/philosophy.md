# Philosophy

AI is useful when it helps people solve real problems with more speed, clarity, and care. It becomes harmful when it is sold as magic, used to create false confidence, or pointed at people through spam.

`ai-offer-starter` exists to help builders design offers that are useful, honest, and deliverable.

## Core Beliefs

- A good offer starts with a real customer, not a tool.
- A good deliverable solves a visible problem, not an abstract trend.
- A good sales process respects attention and consent.
- A good price reflects value, scope, and delivery effort.
- A good AI-assisted service still needs human review.

## Practical Standard

Before selling an offer, you should be able to answer:

- Who is this for?
- What painful or expensive problem does it address?
- What will the customer receive?
- What will you not do?
- How will you validate interest before building?
- How will you deliver and check quality?

## No Magic Claims

The project rejects guaranteed income, passive income claims, and "money printer" language. AI can improve leverage, but it does not guarantee demand, trust, or outcomes.
