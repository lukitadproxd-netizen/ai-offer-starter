# Who This Is For

This toolkit is for people who can create AI-assisted deliverables but need help turning that ability into a responsible service offer.

## Good Fit

- A designer who can create brand kits, landing pages, or content systems.
- A no-code builder who can create automations and internal workflows.
- A writer who can create researched content packages.
- A local consultant who can help businesses respond faster or organize information.
- A data person who can create reports and dashboards.

## Not a Good Fit

This is not for people who want to:

- sell guaranteed income
- automate spam
- scrape private data
- hide AI use from customers
- sell generic "AI transformation" without scope

## Beginner Friendly

You do not need to build a large app. You need one clear customer, one problem, one useful outcome, and one delivery process you can complete well.
