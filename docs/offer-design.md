# Offer Design

An offer is a promise of a specific deliverable for a specific customer with a clear outcome, scope, price, and delivery process.

## Offer Anatomy

A practical offer includes:

- customer
- problem
- outcome
- deliverables
- timeline
- price
- limits
- quality checks
- support window
- next step

## Good Offer Pattern

For `[customer]` who struggle with `[problem]`, I deliver `[deliverables]` so they can `[practical outcome]`, without promising guaranteed revenue.

## Scope Boundaries

Name what is excluded. This protects both sides.

Examples:

- "Does not include ad spend."
- "Does not guarantee sales."
- "Does not include scraping private data."
- "Does not replace legal, financial, or medical advice."
