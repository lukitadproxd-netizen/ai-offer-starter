# Anti-Scam Rules

These rules keep the project practical and safe.

## Hard No

Do not contribute or sell offers that:

- promise guaranteed income
- claim effortless passive income
- use "money printer" positioning
- depend on spam automation
- require scraping private data
- hide material limitations
- imply AI can replace expert judgment in high-risk domains

## Required Clarity

Every offer should state:

- customer
- problem
- deliverables
- price or price range
- timeline
- exclusions
- quality checks
- support window

## Validation

Use `validateOffer()` before publishing an example. Treat errors as blockers and warnings as review prompts.
