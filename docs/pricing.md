# Pricing

Pricing should be simple enough to explain and healthy enough to deliver.

## Basic Structure

Most starter offers can use:

- beta: smaller scope, lower fee, explicit feedback
- standard: normal delivery and support
- pro: expanded scope and recurring improvement

## Setup Fee

The setup fee covers discovery, build time, review, revisions, handoff, and project management.

## Monthly Fee

Use a monthly fee only when there is real recurring work:

- reporting
- updates
- QA
- optimization
- support
- content refreshes

Do not charge recurring fees for nothing. Define the recurring deliverable.

## Unit Economics

Calculate the effective hourly equivalent. If the offer requires many custom hours and produces low effective pay, it may be underpriced freelancing.

Use `generatePricingTiers()` and `calculateSimpleUnitEconomics()` from the core package.
