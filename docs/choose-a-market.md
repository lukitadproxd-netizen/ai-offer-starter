# Choose a Market

Choose a market before choosing a deliverable. A market gives your offer context: customer language, buying triggers, budget, urgency, and delivery expectations.

## Selection Criteria

Pick a market where:

- you can name specific customer types
- customers already spend money to solve related problems
- the problem is visible from public or customer-provided information
- you can reach people without spam
- delivery does not require private data you cannot safely handle

## Good Market Examples

- local accommodation providers
- independent restaurants
- small clinics
- solo consultants
- tourism guides
- local service businesses
- small ecommerce stores

## Avoid

- markets where legal, medical, or financial advice is the core deliverable
- markets where you need sensitive private data before trust exists
- markets where the only channel is bulk unsolicited outreach
